OUR STORY

Founded in 2012, BLAQTRONICS entered the South African ICT market with a focus on network infrastructure for local businesses. Over time, we expanded into heavy equipment rentals, allowing us to meet both digital and physical infrastructure needs.

Backed by experience with leading local and global ICT firms, our skilled team specializes in LAN, radio frequency, and microwave wireless technologies. This expertise supports high-quality deployments across diverse projects.
